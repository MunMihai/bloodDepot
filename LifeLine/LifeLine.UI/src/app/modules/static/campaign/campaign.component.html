1. 2025 Year

    Sustainable Future

    As we look to the future, our commitment remains unwavering.

    We aspire to establish a sustainable blood donation ecosystem that ensures a continuous supply of safe blood for generations to come.

  2. 2024 Year

    Research and Innovation

    2024 has seen us partnering with research institutions to support studies related to blood transfusion and blood-related disorders.

    We aim to contribute to medical advancements in this field.

  3. 2023 Year

    Community Engagement

    Our focus in 2023 has been on building a strong community of donors and supporters.

    We organized awareness campaigns and community events to encourage regular blood donation.

  4. 2022 Year

    Enhanced Technology Integration

    In 2022, we leveraged advanced technology to streamline the donor experience.

    We introduced a user-friendly mobile app for donor registration and appointment scheduling.

  5. 2021 Year

    Expansion and Accessibility

    The year 2021 marked a period of expansion as we extended our services to undeserved regions, ensuring accessibility to blood donation facilities.

    We introduced mobile blood collection units to reach remote communities.

  6. 2020 Year

    Global Impact

    In the year 2020, we reached a remarkable achievement by contributing to the well-being of 10 million people worldwide.

    We provided vital blood and blood products to those in need, saving countless lives in the process.

  7. 2019 Year

    WHO Accreditation

    A significant milestone was achieved in 2019 when our project received accreditation from the World Health Organization (WHO).

    This recognition validated our commitment to maintaining high standards of safety and efficiency in blood collection and distribution.

  8. 2018 Year

    Inception and Vision

    In 2018, our blood donation project was born with a clear vision to make a meaningful impact on healthcare.

    We initiated our mission to bridge the gap in blood supply and demand.
